Leonard Kendall, AICP

Planner & Cartographer

My Name is Leonard Kendall, I am an urban and regional planner in Cincinnati. Originally from Connecticut, I have since lived in Massachusetts and Colorado. My work has been in the public, private, and non-profit sectors of planning so I have experience with a broad sense of the field. I love working to create healthy places where people can enjoy either as residents, workers, or visitors. My work has led me to GIS and Cartography and my goal is to use maps to tell stories in different parts of the world.
